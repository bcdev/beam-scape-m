Scape-M Processing Tool

Summary

The Scape-M Processing Tool provides an atmospheric correction designed for land surfaces, inland waters and coastal water regions. This processor was developed in the frame of the ESA Diversity II project with support of L. Guanter (Free University of Berlin) who developed the underlying algorithm.

Algorithms

The underlying algorithm is presented in detail in the peer-reviewed publication [1].

References

[1] Guanter, L., Gomez-Chova, L., and J. Moreno (2008): Coupled retrieval of aerosol optical thickness, columnar water vapour and surface reflectance maps from ENVISAT/MERIS data over land. Rem. Sens. Environ., 112, 2898-2913.
